## What do you think research is?
Research is the gathering and analysis of information, in order to increase ones understanding of a topic.

## Do you think that you will ever need research skills?
I think having research skills will be incredibly useful in the future. Being able to efficiently collect and analyse information, to gain a better understanding of a research topic is an invaluable skill to possess, and carries over into all aspects of life.

## What do you think a research journal is, and who is it written for?
A research journal is a record kept by the researcher, to allow them to reflect on a particluar area of research which they have studied.
