## What do you think research is?
In it's simplest form, research is the gathering and analysis of information, in order to increase ones understanding of a topic. 

## Do you think that you will ever need research skills?
I think having research skills is incredibly useful. Being able to efficiently and effectively collect and analyse information, to gain a better understanding of a research topic is an invaluable skill to possess, and carries over into all aspects of life.

## What do you think a research journal is, and who is it written for?
A research journal is a record kept by the researcher; it allows them to reflect on a particluar area of research which they have studied. A research journal is written for the author themselves, so they can look back at their research easily, and refresh the knowledge they had gained previously.

## What is plagiarism?
Plagiarism is using someone else's words, language, thoughts or ideas, and not giving them credit for it. In other words, plagiarism is the act of of using someone else's words, and pretending they are your own.

## Why should you avoid it?
There are many reasons to avoid using plagiarism, the most important one being that it is considered fraud. You are using someone else's work, and claiming it as your own. This has various consequences and it should be thoroughly understood that plagiarism is illegal.
